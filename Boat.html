Introducing...

Island Hoppin', a 2018 Lagoon 52 Fly Bridge Luxury Catamaran.

Island Hoppin' was commissioned on November ##, 2017 in the beautiful Virgin Islands. She was built in Bordeaux, France before being moved to St.Thomas, U.S.V.I. for her service. Island Hoppin' was designed to provide families, couples, and friends with the time of their lives exploring the world in luxury. She has the capacity for up to 8 guests who come aboard to experience something that will become unforgetable and unmatched to all in her class. We welcome you to come aboard and experience her sea-worthiness for your self.
